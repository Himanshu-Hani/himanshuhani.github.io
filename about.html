About Me

Hello! I’m Himanshu Hani, currently pursuing a BS-MS in Mathematics and Physics at IISER Mohali.

I am particularly interested in Functional Analysis, Geometric Measure Theory, and Partial Differential Equations. I have spent time working on modern mathematical problems involving Sobolev spaces, weak convergence, trace inequalities, and Hamilton–Jacobi equations.

In Summer 2025, I am working at TIFR CAM under Dr. Debabrata Karmakar and also collaborating with Dr. Cosmin Burtea (France) on nonlinear PDEs and conservation laws. In Summer 2024, I worked under Dr. Debajyoti Chaudhuri on problems in Functional Analysis and elliptic regularity.

Outside of academics, I contribute to the Infinity: Math Club at IISER Mohali and actively participate in outreach activities and seminar discussions.
